The drivers. In total there are 20 drivers on the track. And there are 10 different teams, there are 2 drivers per team. The drivers are very important on the track, they are the ones who drive the cars and ensure that the team gets as many points as possible by finishing as high as possible.

The drivers are important, but the teams are just as important. They check whether the car is still doing well and look at the statistics of the car. But they are very important at the pit stops, when the tires need to be changed. This has to be done as quickly and efficiently as possible so that you don't waste time and falls behind the rest.
