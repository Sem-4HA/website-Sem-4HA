Monaco's race takes place in Europe. Bbout 38 thousand people live in Monaco. And across Europe live about 741,4 Million inhabitants. So far the record holder of this circuit is Max Verstappen. This year (2021) the race will be on May 23. The fastest speed last year (2019) was measured at 290 km/h. The Monaco Grand Prix is often the fifth event of the Formula 1 of the Calendar year.
