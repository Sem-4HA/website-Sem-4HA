Spains race takes place in the city of Barcelona. Barcelona is a city in Spain, about 5,6 Million people live in Barcelona. And across Spain live about 46,9 Million inhabitants. So far the record holder of this circuit is Daniel Ricciardo. This year (2021) the race will be on May 9. The fastest speed last year (2019) was measured at 325 km/h. The Spanish Grand Prix is often the fourth event of the Formula 1 of the Calendar year.
