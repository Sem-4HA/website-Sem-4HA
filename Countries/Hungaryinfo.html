HUNGARIAN GRAND PRIX

Cicuit Length:

4.381 (2.722 mi)

Turns:

14

Number of Laps:

70

First Grand Prix:

1986

Race Distance:

306.63 km

Hungary's race takes place in Budapest. Budapest is the capital of Hungary, about 1,7 Million people live in Budapest. And across Hungary live about 9,7 Million inhabitants. So far the record holder of this circuit is Max Verstappen. This year (2021) the race will be on August 1. The fastest speed last year (2019) was measured at 315 km/h. The Hungarian Grand Prix is often the eleventh event of the Formula 1 of the Calendar year.
